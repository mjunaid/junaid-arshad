On June 7, 2022 US Senators proposed a bill to create regulatory framework for digital assets. The full text of the bill can be found here. The bill has to still go through four committees of jurisdiction before it becomes a law. History has taught us that this process could take some time.

This post, provides a quick summary on the key points discussed in the bill and what they mean for the Crypto industry.
